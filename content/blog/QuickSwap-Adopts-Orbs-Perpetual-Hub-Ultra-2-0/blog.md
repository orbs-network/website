**QuickSwap** has integrated Orbs' **Perpetual Hub Ultra 2.0** to power CeFi-grade perpetual futures trading, rolling out as the default perps infrastructure across QuickSwap chains.

QuickSwap is one of DeFi's longest running DEXs, live since 2020 and the leading trading venue in the Polygon ecosystem. Governed by its dragon community through QUICK token voting, QuickSwap operates across Polygon PoS, and Base, and has partnered with Orbs for years, running dTWAP, dLIMIT, and Liquidity Hub in production.

The integration follows a community governance vote, "Full Shift of Decentralised Perpetuals to Orbs Network," which passed with 81.8% support. Perpetual Hub Ultra now becomes QuickSwap's default perps infrastructure across all chains, replacing the Orderly-powered Falkor deployment on Polygon PoS and building on the Orbs-powered perps platform QuickSwap launched on Base in Q4 2025.

With this integration, **QuickSwap** gains access to a complete, self-contained perps stack: execution, settlement, hedging, liquidation, pricing, and a professional-grade trading UI, all running natively on Orbs' Layer-3 infrastructure with no third-party dependencies. Liquidity is sourced from day one through the platform's integrated hedging infrastructure, so each QuickSwap deployment goes live with deep execution immediately, no bootstrapping required.

The result is a perps trading experience optimized for capital efficiency, execution certainty, and predictable risk even under volatile market conditions.

![](/assets/img/blog/QuickSwap-Adopts-Orbs-Perpetual-Hub-Ultra-2-0/image2.png)

Powered by Orbs' **Layer-3 infrastructure**, this deployment builds on successful integrations of Perpetual Hub Ultra, cementing Orbs as the industry standard for turnkey perps infrastructure in DeFi.

*Orbs is a decentralized infrastructure layer executed by a public network of permissionless validators using dPoS, staked with tens of millions of dollars in total value locked (TVL). Orbs Layer-3 infrastructure optimizes on-chain trading by providing aggregated liquidity, advanced trading orders, and decentralized derivatives, enabling a DeFi trading experience as efficient as CeFi.*

## About Perpetual Hub Ultra 2.0

**Perpetual Hub Ultra 2.0** is a complete, turnkey perpetual futures platform developed by Orbs. It gives any trading venue everything needed to launch high performance perps, built and running natively on Orbs' Layer-3 infrastructure with no third-party dependencies:

- **Full stack, in-house:** execution, settlement, hedging, liquidation, pricing, and a professional-grade trading UI in a single integration layer.
- **Deep liquidity from day one:** trusted, whitelisted hedgers with high-SLA guarantees source liquidity across centralized exchanges, including Binance USD-M futures, and leading on-chain perpetual markets.
- **Verifiable by design:** trades, position updates, and liquidations run through a TEE-secured execution architecture, driven by cryptographically signed price feeds, with state roots committed on-chain via rollup settlement.
- **CeFi-grade experience:** market, limit, stop-loss and take-profit, and advanced bracket orders, plus one-click trading, account abstraction, and gasless flows.

![](/assets/img/blog/QuickSwap-Adopts-Orbs-Perpetual-Hub-Ultra-2-0/image3.jpg)

The result: trading venues, frontends, and aggregators can deploy perps with minimal development effort and fast time to market, competing with centralized exchanges on performance, flexibility, and user experience while retaining the self-custody and transparency of on-chain.

## About Orbs

Orbs is a decentralized Layer-3 (L3) blockchain designed specifically for advanced on-chain trading. Utilizing a Proof-of-Stake consensus, Orbs acts as a supplementary execution layer, facilitating complex logic and scripts beyond the native functionalities of smart contracts.
